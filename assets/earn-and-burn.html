AJIO Wallet Earn and Burn Offer

 

1. What is AJIO wallet Earn and burn offer?

Under this offer, all AJIO customers placing an order worth Rs. 500 or above, will be eligible to get 4% cash back (as AJIO points in AJIO Wallet) which can be used for their future shopping on AJIO. The maximum points that can be earned on an order is Rs 500.

2. How long is the offer valid?

This offer will commence on 5th Aug 2021, 12:00 AM and continue till 15th Aug 2021, 11:59 PM.

3. When will I get the cashback amount?

The AJIO Points earned will be added to the Customer’s AJIO Wallet after 30 days from the delivery of the Specified Product. Once credited, the AJIO Points will be valid for next 30 days.

4. How can I redeem the cashback amount?

Once live, Customers can redeem AJIO Points at 100% value for a period of 30 days. These AJIO Points will be applicable on all orders worth Rs. 500 and above. However, after 30 days, the AJIO Points will become inactive and would no longer be available for use.

5. What happens if I cancel/return an order partially or completely?

In case of cancellation or return of orders, the AJIO Points will be made inactive and can no longer be used by the Customer.

6. Facing issues in availing the offer?

If customers are facing any issues regarding cashback, customers can contact AJIO at [https://www.ajio.com/selfcare]
